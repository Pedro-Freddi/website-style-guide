III. Headings

The different types of headings and their styles are defined in this section.

Main heading: h1

  • font-family: Raleway
  • font-weight: 700
  • font-size: 24px
  • text-decoration: underline

Subheading: h2

  • font-family: Raleway
  • font-weight: 700
  • font-size: 22px
  • font-style: italic

Subheading: h3

  • font-family: Raleway
  • font-weight: 400
  • font-size: 20px
  • font-style: italic

Subheading: h4

  • font-family: "Open Sans"
  • font-weight: 400
  • font-size: 18px

V. Links

The style guides for links are defined in this section.

General Style

  • color: #060e9e;
  • text-decoration: underline;

Hover Style

  • color: black;
  • background-color: #6bc7e3;

Active Style

  • font-size: 1.1em;
  • background-color: #d91c2c;
  • color: white;

Example: I am a link! Hover me and then click me
